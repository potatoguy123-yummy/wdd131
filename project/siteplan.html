BuildTech Supply - Site Plan

1. Site Name

Site Name: BuildTech Supply

Description: This name represents a place for contractors or hobbiests to get tech or materials for construction related projects.

2. Site Purpose

The primary focus is to search and order parts. A contact page should be available for customer support.

3. Scenarios

Some scenarios may be:

  • "I'm a new electrician; what are the essential tools and safety equipment I need to start my business?"
  • "I'm building a deck; what type of lumber is best suited for outdoor use, and what are the current prices?"

4. Color Schema

A dark brown (#683523) for the headers, a pale yellow (#FDF8E2) for the background.

5. Typography

The typography selection balances readability with a modern aesthetic.

Heading - Roboto, Product titles - Roboto Slab, Product specs/all other text - Open Sans
